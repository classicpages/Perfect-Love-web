About Us

Since July 2009, Perfect Love International has empowered youth across the globe to live purpose-driven lives by delivering transformative programs that cultivate leadership capacities, reimagine life’s possibilities, and expand their opportunities for positive impact.

Vision

• To equip and develop people for love, life and leadership.

Mission

• To equip and empower individuals, churches, organizations and communities through the development, hosting and promotion of programs that encourage and foster love.

Aim

• To develop young people into responsible leaders and peer counsellors.

• We seek to help them understand, recognize and promote the right leadership qualities in them and others. Influential leadership, that has the potential to help influence their colleagues positively.

• We seek to identify and develop a class of high potential leaders within the society in terms of effective succession planning.

Events

MAKE A DIFFERENCE Conference

An annual camp meeting to help the youth develop competence in life skills, enhance their leadership abilities and help discover themselves through; life activities, teamwork, talk sessions, educational trips, fun games and others.

Events

MAD CREATIVE FESTIVAL

It is a two-day program, which is climaxed with a night of creativity, where talents are expressed through performing arts such as: 1.Music 2.Dance 3.Drama 4.Poetry 5.Paintings

Camp Life Jacket

An immersive camp experience that leverages on engaging activities such as leadership workshops and team bonding spaces to boost capacity building, teamwork and drive measurable improvements in collaboration and performance.

A Love Story

This is a yearly organized community-based program, which aims at fostering good and cordial relationship among individuals through the promotion of peace and harmony. Lessons on healthy family life and solid relationships are also learnt though this program.

Raised to Reign

Raised to Reign is a program designed for students. Perfect Love International has a team of professionals who shares rich experiences with the students, through motivational talks on academics, social life and health and a musical and drama night to crown the program.
