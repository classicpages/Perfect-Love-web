About Us

Since July 2009, Perfect Love International has empowered youth across the globe to live purpose-driven lives by delivering transformative programs that cultivate leadership capacities, reimagine life’s possibilities, and expand their opportunities for positive impact.

Vision

To equip and develop people for love, life and leadership.

Mission

To equip and empower individuals, churches, organizations and communities through the development, hosting and promotion of programs that encourage and foster love.
